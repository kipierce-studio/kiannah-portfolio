Project-Based Learning in History and Social Studies Class for Middle Schoolers

This project was completed in UNC's Education 915 Learning Sciences Course. This project aimed to find a new way to teach subject matter to students. An Athlete's Guide to Life After Sport

As my final capstone project for the Masters of Applied Professional Studies at UNC, I worked with members of UNC Athletic's Support Staff to design a tool-kit for those that graduate or retire from sport. This guide covered a variety of topics to better prepare them for 'real-life' struggles.
